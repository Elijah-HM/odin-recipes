Egg and Sausage Wrap

Quick and Easy Breakfast Wrap

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Tortilla Wrap
  • Butter or butter substitute
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook sausage in skillet using butter or substitute
  2. Remove sausage from skillet and scramble eggs in same pan
  3. Add eggs and sausage to tortilla and wrap it
  4. Add butter or substitute to new pan and toast wrap on both sides for 2mins on med heat
  5. Serve
