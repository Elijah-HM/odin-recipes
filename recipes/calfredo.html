Chicken Alfredo

Who doesnt love a plate of Chicken Alfredo?

Ingredients

  • 3 pack of chicken breast
  • Fettuccine noodles
  • 1 1/2 cans Ragu Alfredo sauce
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Add chicken to skillet and cook until (180 degrees F) Add whatever seasonings you like. Cut chicken into pieces
  2. Bring Fettuccine noodles to a boil and drain when cooked (fork test)
  3. Add noodles, chicken, black pepper, and alfredo sauce to the pot you cooked noodles in and then stir
