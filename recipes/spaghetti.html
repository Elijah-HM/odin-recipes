Eazey make Spaghetti Recipe

A super easy Spaghetti recipe that will taste better than Olive Garderns

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 can spaghetti sauce (Ragu)
  • 1 box angel hair noodles
  • shredded cheese (Sharp shredded)
  • 1 tablespoons oregano

Steps

  1. Bring angel hair noodles to boil, cook ground beef in pot until brown and then drain the fat from meat and water from noodles
  2. Add 1 can of spaghetti sauce, 2 tablespoon of sugar, and 1 tablespoon of oregano to the meat then Stir
  3. Add serving sizes of noodles to plate, add the meat and cheese on top
  4. Serve
